Mission Statement:

Our mission at Apart and Together is to provide as many people as possible information and resources that relate to COVID-19 because everyone needs these resources to stay healthy.

Why Apart + Together?

We chose the name 'Apart + Together' because we believed this was the best way to describe the current situation. Althoug we are quarantine, and apart from each other, we still want people to have a way to connect together. This website will help people stay educated on COVID-19, so that we can flatten that curve, and go back to being with each other again.
